Design System

André Bowen — MD3

Material Design 3 applied to a painting portfolio. Neutral monochrome scheme. Artwork images always render with square corners regardless of component context. All tokens, components, and interactive states documented below.

01

28-role MD3 color system. Full dark scheme auto-activates via prefers-color-scheme: dark. Primary is #1d1d1f — near-black — mapping cleanly to a neutral monochrome palette.
